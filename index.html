FAQ

How does I get new enrolments?

As a Footprints teacher, parents will enrol in two ways:

- Digital marketing campaigns run by Footprints
- By you advertising in your apartment society through whatsapp, notice boards, putting a canopy

What kind of training will I get as a teacher?

There will be 3 weeks training before starting the operations on curriculum, technology, parent interaction.

what do i need to have?

You need to be a mother, living in an apartment complex. You must be someone who loves children and speak english.

What does my investment go?

You will invest money in making your house child safe and buy toys, cots, utensils, stuff to be able to conduct children activities.

will there be a CCTV installed in my house?

Yes, it will be installed at the children play area and entrance. You will have control to switch it off once children leave your home.
